About Jani

Jani Orbinski (TeM) is a finnish sound designer, composer, and sound artist who transforms stories into unforgettable sonic journeys. With a deep understanding of RHYTHM and FLOW, Jani uses his musical roots to create soundscapes that pulse with ENERGY and EMOTION, elevating the listener’s experience.

From crafting gripping audio for radio plays and theater productions to composing evocative music that stands alone or complements adaptive soundscapes for games, Jani brings PRECISION, CREATIVITY, and a PASSION for innovation to every project. His expertise shines in his ability to seamlessly blend storytelling with sound, creating works that resonate on a gut level.

In the realm of sound art, Jani embraces technology as a creative partner. By leveraging platforms like Max/MSP and Python, he develops generative and interactive audio experiences that push artistic boundaries and invite audiences to ENGAGE with SOUND in new ways.
